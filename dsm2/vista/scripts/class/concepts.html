Important conceptual terms:

1. Data Reference:
    A reference to a data set, its location ( local or internet address), its filename, its pathname and the data set it refers.

2. Time Series
    A sampled time series with each element of such a series consisting of a unique time stamp and a value. If the sampling is done at regular intervals it is called a Regular Time Series and if the sampling is at irregular intervals it is called an Irregular Time Series
